MSD Content Management System

Welcome to the Medical Stores Department Content Management System (MSD-CMS)! This system empowers administrators to efficiently manage and streamline essential content for Medical Stores Department (MSD) Website.

Main User: Administrator
Functions: Seamlessly create, update, and organize pages, manage images and files, customize settings, and ensure critical content is always up-to-date.

As the admin, you are the backbone of this system, ensuring smooth operations and making data accessible to the website. Your role is pivotal in keeping the system up to date for the betterment of the company's future
